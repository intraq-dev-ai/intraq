# Code of Conduct

We want intraQ spaces to be practical, respectful, and useful.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, but keep discussion evidence-based.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the work.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect user data, credentials, and private operational information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, abuse, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally derailing technical discussion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations
may result in being blocked from project spaces.
